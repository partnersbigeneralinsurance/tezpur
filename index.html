Our General Insurance Services

We provide a wide range of general insurance services to protect you and your assets. Our offerings include:

  • Motor Insurance: Comprehensive coverage for vehicles, including third-party liability, own damage, and add-ons like roadside assistance.
  • Health Insurance: Plans for individuals, families, and groups, covering hospitalization, medical expenses, and preventive care.
  • Property Insurance: Protection for homes, businesses, and assets against fire, theft, and natural disasters.
  • Travel Insurance: Coverage for trips, including medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost baggage.
  • Personal Accident Insurance: Financial protection in case of accidental injuries or death.
  • Liability Insurance: Covers legal liabilities for businesses and individuals.
  • Marine Insurance: Protection for goods in transit by sea, air, or land.
  • Fire and Allied Perils Insurance: Safeguards against fire, explosion, and related risks.
  • Engineering Insurance: Covers machinery, equipment, and construction projects.
  • Customized Policies: Tailored insurance solutions based on your specific needs.

Claims Support: In case of claims, we provide full service and support to our customers, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free process.
